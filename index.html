Mission and Vision

The mission of the Department of Computer Systems Engineering is to prepare undergraduate and graduate students for productive careers in industry, academia, and government by providing an outstanding environment for teaching, learning, and research in the theory and applications of computing. The Department places high priority on establishing and maintaining innovative research programs to enhance its education quality and achieve excellence in state-of-the-art research trends.


Message from Chairman

Department of Computer Systems Engineering, as per its mission, strives to develop and maintain a high-quality, comprehensive, rigorous and accredited teaching program in Computer Engineering to prepare graduates of competence, conscience, and compassion, who are prepared to excel in the field of computing.

In order to achieve this goal, we combine the theoritical foundations of computing with the practical engineering knowledge vital to industry, to provide a broad and integrated curriculum.
